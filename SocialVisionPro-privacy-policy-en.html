1. Introduction

At Social Vision Pro ("App", "We", "Us"), protecting our users' privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Instagram Data

Our app collects the following data through Instagram API:

  • Profile Information: Username, profile picture, bio information
  • Follower Data: Follower list, following users
  • Statistics: Post count, story view counts
  • Interaction Data: Like, comment, share counts

2.3 Cloud Data Transmission

Our app sends the following data to cloud services:

  • Instagram Username: Only username (not password)
  • Anonymous Device Information: Device model, operating system
  • Anonymous Statistics: App usage data
  • Error Reports: App errors (contains no personal data)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Analytics Services: To analyze your Instagram account performance
  • Follower Management: To track follower changes
  • AutoPilot Features: For automatic social media activities
  • App Development: To improve our services

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties. Your data is only shared:

  • Securely with Instagram API
  • With authorized authorities when legally required

5. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • 256-bit Encryption: Your Instagram passwords are stored on your device with 256-bit encryption
  • Local Storage: Your passwords are stored only on your device and never sent externally
  • SSL Encryption: All data transfers are encrypted
  • Anonymous Data: Data sent to cloud is anonymized
  • Secure Database: Secure data storage with SQLite
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access
  • Regular Backup: Secure backup of data

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request access to your data
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of incorrect data

Our app is designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
